## What HOSTit Is Missing (vs Planning Pod)
1. **Dashboard/Home** - Overview stats: upcoming events, pending enquiries, revenue this month, tasks due
2. **Client Portal** - Shareable link where clients can view their event details, approve proposals, sign contracts
3. **Contracts with e-Signatures** - Digital contract creation and signing (currently only proposals)
4. **Budgeting Module** - Event budget tracker with categories, actual vs estimated costs
5. **Task Manager** - Cross-event task management with due dates, assignees, priorities
6. **Email Communications Hub** - Centralised inbox for all client communications per booking
7. **Contacts/CRM** - Dedicated contacts database (clients, vendors, staff) separate from bookings
8. **Equipment Management** - Track AV, furniture, linen, equipment per event
9. **Seating Charts** - Visual drag-and-drop table/seat assignment
10. **File Sharing** - Upload and share files per event (contracts, menus, run sheets)
11. **Webforms/Lead Capture** - Embeddable enquiry forms for venue website
12. **Payment Tracking** - Deposit tracking, payment reminders, installment plans
13. **Reporting/Analytics** - Revenue reports, booking trends, occupancy rates
14. **Multi-space Management** - Multiple rooms/spaces with individual availability
15. **Staff Management** - Staff roster, assignments per event
